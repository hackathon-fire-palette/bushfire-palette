About This Website

This is a demonstration bushfire alert system for Australia, inspired by the Australian Warning System and the GDACS Alerts dashboard. It uses the Australian Fire Danger Rating System (AFDRS) and official warning icons/colors.

Disclaimer

This website is for demonstration purposes only and not for operational use. For real-time bushfire information, visit your state or territory emergency service website or australianwarningsystem.com.au.

How Alerts Work

  • Alerts are categorized by severity: Advice, Watch and Act, Emergency Warning.
  • Severity is based on the AFDRS and may use mock data for demonstration.
  • Recommended actions are provided for each alert level.
